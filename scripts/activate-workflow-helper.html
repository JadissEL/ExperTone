Activate Expert Hunter Workflow

Your platform calls https://ecosbuilder.app.n8n.cloud/webhook/hunt. That URL only works when the workflow is active.

  1. Click the button below to open your workflow in n8n.
  2. In the top-right of the editor, find the Active toggle.
  3. Turn it ON (it should turn blue/green).
  4. Important: Make a small change (e.g. add a space in a note) and press Ctrl+S (or Cmd+S) to save. This can help n8n register the production webhook.
